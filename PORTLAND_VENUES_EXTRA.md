## Additional Community & Event Spaces

### Water Avenue Coffee — 1300 SW 5th Ave
- **Distance:** ~0.4 km (Downtown)
- **Type:** Café (casual meetup spot)
- **Why it's great:** Spacious café with good Wi-Fi; perfect for small informal security discussion groups and brown-bag sessions; coffee-fueled community hub
- **Capacity:** 15–30 people (standing/table-style)
- **Cost:** Free (purchase beverages); reservation may be needed for groups > 15

### Pioneer Courthouse Square — SW Taylor & SW Yamhill
- **Distance:** ~0.3 km (Downtown core)
- **Type:** Public square (outdoor gathering space)
- **Why it's great:** Iconic "Portland's Living Room"; outdoor events and public talks possible; connected to Pioneer Place for weather backup; great visibility for community announcements
- **Capacity:** 100–500 (outdoor event); ~200 (indoor near Pavilion)
- **Cost:** Free for public use; permit may be required for organized events

### Dossier Hotel — 750 SW Alder St
- **Distance:** ~0.4 km (Downtown)
- **Type:** Boutique hotel with event spaces
- **Why it's great:** Modern event rooms; upscale environment for sponsor-branded security conferences; hotel rooms for out-of-town attendees
- **Capacity:** 50–150 (meeting rooms)
- **Cost:** Rental fee applies; hotel rates for overnight guests

### UO Portland Center — NE Holman Street (Concordia)
- **Distance:** ~1.8 km
- **Type:** University extension venue (University of Oregon)
- **Why it's great:** Affordable room rentals; academic environment for workshops; capable of hosting moderate-sized groups
- **Capacity:** 30–80 (classroom/lecture style)
- **Cost:** Low-cost community rental through UO Professional and Continuing Education

### Coffee Time — 712 NW 21st Ave (Nob Hill)
- **Distance:** ~1.0 km (Northwest District)
- **Type:** Café
- **Why it's great:** Neighbourhood café with a cozy atmosphere; good for small team offsites and planning sessions; strong Wi-Fi
- **Capacity:** 10–25

### Lloyd Center — 2201 Lloyd Center (Lloyd District)
- **Distance:** ~2.0 km
- **Type:** Shopping mall with event/corner spaces
- **Why it's great:** Large indoor space for pop-up events and demo days; accessible transit (MAX alignment); parking available
- **Capacity:** Variable (can host small pop-up events)

### Oregon Community Foundation — 1221 SW Yamhill St
- **Distance:** ~0.3 km (Downtown)
- **Type:** Community foundation / nonprofit
- **Why it's great:** Nonprofit community hub; mission-aligned with the security initiative; meeting rooms for collaborative workshops
- **Capacity:** 20–60
- **Cost:** Historically offers community space at reduced or no cost for community-building work
