# Privacy Notice for Web Security Community Events

_A template privacy notice adapted from clinical-trial informed-consent principles (NCT05129397, NCT06804967 verified via ClinicalTrials.gov API). Replace bracketed sections before use._

---

**Web Security Community Initiative** ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

## What Data We Collect

| Category | Examples | Purpose | Retention |
|---|---|---|---|
| Contact info | Name, email, phone number | Event registration, post-event follow-up | 3 years after last event attendance |
| Attendance records | Date(s) attended, workshop topics | Aggregate reporting, grant reporting | 3 years |
| Photo or video (opt-in only) | Headshots, event photos, recorded sessions | Publicity, social media, website content | Until explicitly deleted via request |
| Accessibility needs | Dietary restrictions, mobility requests | Venue accommodation | 1 year |
| Survey responses | Feedback, cybersecurity experience level | Program improvement | 2 years |

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Social security numbers or government ID
- Payment card numbers (we redirect to Stripe/TicketTailor if any fee applies)
- Biometric or health data (mirrors clinical-trial exclusion; no fitness trackers or health questions unless explicitly part of a research study with IRB approval)

## Legal Basis

We process your personal data under **legitimate interest** (community event coordination) and **consent** (photo/video, newsletter sign-up). You have the right to withdraw consent at any time by emailing `privacy@websecurity.community`.

## Third-Party Processors

| Processor | Purpose | Region (Data Residency) |
|---|---|---|
| Google Workspace (or equivalent) | RSVP & mailing | US (EFTA-approved SCCs in place) |
| Zoom / Element (or equivalent E2E conferencing platform with HIPAA-grade encryption) | Virtual attendance | US / EU (configurable) |
| Mailchimp (or equivalent) | Email newsletter | US (EFTA-approved SCCs) |
| OSM / Nominatim | Portland venue geocoding (public, read-only) | Global |

## Your Rights

- **Access:** Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
- **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data.
- **Erasure:** Request deletion via email. We comply within 30 days.
- **Portability:** Receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).
- **Deletion of photos/video:** Send a photo description (date + what you're wearing) to `privacy@websecurity.community`; we will purge matching media within 30 days.

## Security Measures

- All electronic storage encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3).
- Role-based access: Admin (3 people), Moderator (5–10 per event), Attendee (public).
- 2FA required for admin account holders.
- Annual third-party penetration test of community infrastructure.

## Data Breach Notification

We will notify affected parties within **72 hours** of a confirmed breach (mirroring GDPR Art. 33 and HIPAA Breach Notification Rule principles). Notification will include: what happened, what data was affected, steps taken, and how to protect yourself.
