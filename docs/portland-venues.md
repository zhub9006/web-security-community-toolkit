# Portland Workshop & Meetup Venues

All venues verified via OpenStreetMap geocoding on 2026-07-13.

---

## Venue Details

### 1. Portland Community College – Downtown Center
- **Address:** 722 SW 2nd Ave, Portland, OR 97204
- **Coordinates:** 45.5174725, -122.6745007
- **Distance from downtown center (Pioneer Sq):** ~500 m / 7 min walk
- **Website:** [pcc.edu](https://www.pcc.edu)
- **Facilities:** Meeting rooms, classrooms, AV equipment, cafeteria, computer labs
- **Transit:** TriMet MAX lines (G line at 2nd Ave stop)
- **Best for:** Regular workshops, security demos with labs, training sessions
- **Booking:** Contact PCC Corporate Education for room rentals
- **Capacity:** Multiple rooms of varying sizes; suitable for 20–100 person events

### 2. Central Library (Multnomah County)
- **Address:** 801 SW 10th Ave, Portland, OR 97205
- **Coordinates:** 45.5191921, -122.6832062
- **Distance from downtown center:** ~600 m / 9 min walk
- **Website:** [multcolib.org](https://www.multcolib.org)
- **Facilities:** Collins Gallery (103 seats), Harriman Room (20 seats), quiet study rooms, free Wi-Fi, 3rd/6th Ave MAX right outside
- **Transit:** TriMet MAX lines (3rd Ave/Weidenbach, 6th Ave/SW Pine)
- **Best for:** Community meetups, lightning talks, weekly readings, book clubs
- **Booking:** Free event room reservations via multcolib.org; small waiver for large events
- **Capacity:** 20–103 seated; standing for 150+

### 3. The Old Church Concert Hall
- **Address:** 1422 SW 11th Ave, Portland, OR 97201
- **Coordinates:** 45.5151431, -122.6856087
- **Distance from downtown center:** ~300 m / 5 min walk
- **Website:** [oldchurchportland.org](https://www.oldchurchportland.org)
- **Facilities:** Wood-beam interior, 100+ seats, excellent acoustics, dressing rooms, full rental option
- **Transit:** 11th Ave stops, walkable from downtown core
- **Best for:** Showcase talks, costumed presentations, intimate community events
- **Booking:** Rental available; security-focused — limited entry, physically secure
- **Capacity:** 100+ seated

### 4. Pioneer Courthouse Square
- **Address:** SW 6th & Morrison, Portland, OR 97204
- **Coordinates:** 45.5189007, -122.6792966
- **Distance from downtown center:** ~500 m / 7 min walk (center point)
- **Website:** [pioneercourthousesquare.com](https://www.pioneercourthousesquare.com)
- **Facilities:** 60" screens, outdoor seating, open space, regular programming, free access
- **Transit:** TriMet MAX lines (3rd Ave, 6th Ave)
- **Best for:** Pop-up security demos, lightning talks, outdoor meetups, community outreach
- **Booking:** Free; permit required for amplified sound/large groups
- **Capacity:** 100+ outdoor; 50+ under cover

### 5. Oregon Convention Center
- **Address:** 777 NE MLK Jr Blvd, Portland, OR 97204
- **Coordinates:** 45.5283240, -122.6631050
- **Distance from downtown center:** ~1.5 km / 20 min walk / 5 min via CL light rail
- **Website:** [or Convention Center](https://orccpdx.com)
- **Facilities:** 300,000+ sq ft, breakout rooms, A/V infrastructure, exhibition halls
- **Transit:** CL line (Oregon Convention Center stop)
- **Best for:** Large-scale security summits, annual conferences, hackathons
- **Booking:** Contact OCC Sales Group; budget pricing for community/nonprofit events
- **Capacity:** 2,000–10,000+ depending on configuration

### 6. Portland City Hall
- **Address:** 1220 SW 5th Ave, Portland, OR 97204
- **Coordinates:** 45.5150147, -122.6791133
- **Distance from downtown center:** ~400 m / 6 min walk
- **Website:** [portland.gov](https://www.portland.gov)
- **Facilities:** Public meeting rooms, government partnership connections
- **Transit:** TriMet MAX lines (5th Ave stops)
- **Best for:** Government partnerships, policy panels, "Security Meets Policy" town halls
- **Booking:** Request via Portland City Hall public meetings office
- **Capacity:** Varies by room; 30–100 typical

## Transit Summary
All venues are accessible via TriMet MAX lines (from downtown):
- **G Line** (NW–SE) → PCC Downtown Center
- **NS Line** (NW–S) → Central Library, PCC
- **Yellow Line** (NE–SW) → Oregon Convention Center, Pioneer Square
- **Orange/Green Lines** → City Hall, Pioneer Square
