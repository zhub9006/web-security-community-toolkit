## Booking Tips
- **Multnomah County libraries**: Free for non-profits/community groups; request 4–6 weeks ahead.
- **Portland State University**: Classroom rental through the PSU Bookings office; rates are nonprofit-friendly.
- **Oregon Convention Center**: Contact events team for multi-track security conferences; CTF/pentest-friendly infrastructure available.
- **Revolution Hall**: Affordable venue hire for weekend hackathons; capacity ~600.
