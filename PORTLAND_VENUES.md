## Pioneer Courthouse Square

- **Address:** 701 SW 6th Avenue, Portland, OR 97204
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0 km (at the heart of downtown)
- **Type:** Public Square
- **Why it works:** Portland's "living room" — an open-air public square that regularly hosts community gatherings, markets, film screenings, and public events. Free to use for outdoor activities.
- **Best For:** Outdoor meetups, startup pitch nights, casual security chats
- **Notes:** Weather-dependent; check city permits for amplified sound or large gatherings

---

## Portland Art Museum

- **Address:** 1219 SW Park Avenue, Portland, OR 97205
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0.05 km (~1 min walk)
- **Type:** Museum / Event Venue
- **Why it works:** One of Portland's premier event venues with private galleries and event spaces. The building itself is a landmark on Park Avenue with strong community presence.
- **Best For:** Sponsor-branded events, elegant workshops, networking mixers
- **Notes:** Private event rental rates apply; contact the museum's events team

---

## Portland State University

- **Address:** 1825 SW Broadway, Portland, OR 97201
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0.05 km (~1 min walk)
- **Type:** University / Public Venue
- **Why it works:** PSU has extensive event spaces, auditoriums, and classrooms that are sometimes available for community and nonprofit use.
- **Best For:** Larger workshops, hackathons, panel discussions, lectures
- **Notes:** Check PSU's community events office for room availability and rates

---

## Davies Family Research Library

- **Address:** 1200 SW Park Avenue, Portland, OR 97205
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0.01 km (on PSU campus)
- **Type:** Library
- **Why it works:** Research library located directly on the PSU campus in downtown Portland. Quiet spaces suitable for small workshops and focused working sessions.
- **Best For:** Small coding workshops, privacy study groups, intimate meetups (10–20 people)
- **Notes:** Library hours and access may be limited to PSU affiliates

---

## Portland Community College – Downtown Center

- **Address:** 722 SW 2nd Avenue, Portland, OR 97204
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0.7 km (~2 min drive / 15 min walk)
- **Type:** College / Community Venue
- **Why it works:** PCC's downtown location offers affordable room rentals and a strong focus on community and technology education. Well-equipped with projectors, Wi-Fi, and flexible seating.
- **Best For:** Educational workshops, training sessions, beginner-friendly security classes
- **Notes:** Competitive booking; contact PCC downtown center for room rates

---

## Native American Student & Community Center

- **Address:** 710 SW Jackson Street, Portland, OR 97201
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~0.9 km (~2 min drive / 18 min walk)
- **Type:** Community Centre
- **Why it works:** A dedicated community centre on the PSU campus that hosts cultural, educational, and civic events. Welcoming atmosphere with flexible event spaces.
- **Best For:** Inclusive community meetups, roundtable discussions, film screenings
- **Notes:** Confirm community rental policies and pricing

---

## Helfgott Research Institute & Community Education Center

- **Address:** 2220 SW 1st Avenue, Portland, OR 97201
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~1.1 km (~2 min drive / 20 min walk)
- **Type:** Educational / Community Venue
- **Why it works:** Hosted by the National College of Natural Medicine, this center offers community education spaces. Well-suited for workshops that combine technical and community-building elements.
- **Best For:** Interactive workshops, hands-on security exercises, community education series
- **Notes:** Check with NCNM for current rental availability

---

## Oregon Convention Center

- **Address:** 777 NE Martin Luther King Jr Boulevard, Portland, OR 97204
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~1.7 km (~5 min drive / 25 min walk)
- **Type:** Conference Centre
- **Why it works:** Portland's largest dedicated event venue with massive flexible space, modern AV infrastructure, catering services, and excellent public transit access.
- **Best For:** Major conferences, large-scale security workshops, city-wide meetups
- **Notes:** High rental cost; best suited for events with 100+ attendees or significant sponsorship

---

## Soho House Portland

- **Address:** 1025 SE Pine Street, Portland, OR 97214
- **Distance from Downtown Core:** ~2.0 km (~5 min drive / 20 min walk)
- **Type:** Social Club / Event Space
- **Why it works:** Modern, tech-friendly environment with private event rooms, stylish common areas, and good amenities. Designed for creative professionals and community gatherings.
- **Best For:** Networking mixers, after-hours meetups, curated speaker events
- **Notes:** Membership may be required or guest fees apply; check current membership policies

## Additional Venues in the Broader Downtown Area

| 100+ people | Oregon Convention Center |
