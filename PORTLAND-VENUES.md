## Key Takeaways for Venue Selection

1. **PCC Downtown Center** is the top pick for structured workshops (classrooms, AV, affordable rates)
2. **Central Library** offers free meeting rooms for smaller community gatherings
3. **The Armory** provides a professional Pearl District setting for presentations
4. **NW 23rd Ave cafés** (Portal Tea, Case Study) are ideal for casual meetups with WiFi
5. **Oregon Convention Center** is the go-to for annual conferences (200+ attendees)
