## Recommended Venue Types by Use Case

| Use Case | Best Venue Choices |
|----------|-------------------|
| **Formal workshop (50+ attendees)** | PCC Downtown Center, PSU lecture halls, Central Library meeting rooms |
| **Tech talk / presentation** | Pioneer Courthouse Square (outdoor), The Armory, Arlene Schnitzer Concert Hall |
| **Casual meetup / networking** | Holocene, Case Study Coffee, Portal Tea, NW 23rd Ave cafés |
| **Small working group** | Central Library meeting rooms, Selling Building, Care Oregon |
| **Large conference (200+)** | Oregon Convention Center (Lloyd District), The Armory |
| **Weekend hands-on workshop** | PCC Downtown Center, PSU classrooms, North Portland Eagles |

## Venue Selection Tips

1. **PCC Downtown Center** is the top pick for structured workshops (classrooms, AV, affordable rates)
2. **Central Library** offers free meeting rooms for smaller community gatherings — book early
3. **Pioneer Courthouse Square** is ideal for free public events and outdoor demos
4. **The Armory** provides a professional Pearl District setting with A/V for presentations
5. **Holocene** is a go-to for informal tech meetups and security talks
6. **NW 23rd Ave cafés** (Portal Tea, Case Study) are great for casual brown-bag sessions
7. **Oregon Historical Society** offers a unique cultural setting for keynote events
